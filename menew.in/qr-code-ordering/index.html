QR CODE ORDERING

Gautam Sharma

A sophisticated and advanced approach to ordering, and yet so simple and convenient in its design. Customers get to order without having to worry about who or what they’re coming in contact with at your restaurant.

Times are rapidly changing. The world is adjusting and adapting as per the new and possible safety and hygiene standards that will follow post Covid-19, and a complete transformation of lifestyle is underway. It’s safe to say that going digital is slowly becoming the new normal, and we can see an exponential growth of technology-based strategies being applied to tackle post-lockdown challenges.


The food and beverage industry is not far behind. Customers will become wary and anxious about their future restaurant visits, and so it is essential for all the restaurant owners to look for a way to deliver a safe experience for their customers. Sanitization is absolutely important, and restauranteurs are taking necessary steps to do so,  but what could be a more permanent, hassle-free solution to a situation like this?

 

Restaurant owners and diners mutually agree upon having a digitized experience in order to maintain maximum safety. And we have been working on creating an end-to-end solution so that you continue to give exceptional services to your customers, while also following the strict health policies that will follow post lockdown. Since digital is the new way to go, Menew brings to you a contactless and digital platform that follows a minimal-touch approach when ordering food.

Welcome to the world of menew QR Code Digital Ordering.

A sophisticated and advanced approach to ordering, and yet so simple and convenient in its design. Customers get to order without having to worry about who or what they’re coming in contact with at your restaurant. Customer-waiter contact is brought down to an absolute minimum for you to be able to maintain maximum safety. Your restaurant will become a space for them to rejoice and have a good time- because you’re making sure that their well-being is your priority.

 

And helping you run your business effectively is ours.

 

 With QR Code Digital Ordering, not only do they feel independent and more in control by ordering on their own, but they also feel more comfortable due to the safety standards being met by your restaurant. This gives you more leverage as you’re providing your customers with the security and assurance they need, and ultimately helps you in increasing customer return frequency.

QR Code Digital Ordering for Dine-in

Step into the future of dining! With Menew you can not only give your customers a futuristic ordering experience, but you’re also ensuring maximum safety by reducing customer-staff contact to its bare minimum.

How it works

  • The customer comes to your restaurant and gets situated at a table
  • She scans the QR code already displayed on the table and unlocks the Menew experience.
  • She gets access to a smart and attractive menu and can order food on her own. If required, she can call the waiter by just pressing a button on the Menew Dining App. She can easily edit her orders- give your customers full control!
  • Once the order is placed, the kitchen receives a digital order ticket, thus reducing paperwork as well as contact with others.
  • Once she’s finished her lovely meal, she makes the payment, which can be done completely online using online modes of payment.
  • She gives her valuable feedback on the Menew App which you can utilize to understand your customers’ preferences and choices!
And there you have it! A hassle-free, paperless dining experience for both you and the customer that they’ll keep coming back to!

And there’s more!

Introducing QR Code Takeaway Ordering!

Give your customers a platform that allows them to order takeaway with just a few clicks! The Menew Website makes takeaway ordering super easy and extremely convenient. From a variety of pickup preferences to choose from, your customers will rush to your restaurant to get the ultimate ordering experience.

How it works

  • The customer reaches your restaurant and scans the QR code- which can either be at your restaurant counter or drive-in. OR The customer will open your restaurant website www.your-restaurant.com  and choose his pick up preference-
    • I’m already at Location (when they’re already at the restaurant)
    • I will pick up my order at (they can specify the time at which they’ll arrive and pick the item)
    • I will reach in (They can give an approximate time by which they’ll come to the restaurant)
      NOTE- The customer can also specify the license plate number and get his food delivered to him by a staff member at his door!
  • He opens the Menew App and places his order.
  • After placing the order, the kitchen receives it and an order ticket is generated, which is sent to both the customer as well as the restaurant.
  • He pays the bill using online modes of payment- hello, cashless ordering!
  • He then collects the order or gets it delivered to this vehicle- maintaining minimal contact at all times!
  • He gives his feedback and enjoys his meal!!!
And voila!

With Menew, you are enabling your customers to take control of their safety as well, and you are making sure they know that you truly care. The future of smart restaurant management is here!

So go ahead, become your customers’ hero by ensuring MINIMUM CONTACT- MAXIMUM SAFETY.
